About Us

Colton Wyllie, co-founder of TuneUp. I played NCAA golf for four years at Lindenwood University and have been a professional golfer for six years. I’ve served as a teaching professional at Angus Glen Golf Club since 2018 which has allowed me to work alongside some of Canada’s leading teaching professionals.

Our Mission

At TuneUp, our mission is to make instruction accessible from anywhere. Whether through virtual lessons or in-person coaching, our team is always available. Our platform enables seamless communication between clients and coaches, facilitating quick and effective analysis of their games.
