Privacy Policy for TuneUp LLC

Effective Date: August 15th, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to TuneUp LLC ("we," "our," or "us"). Our app provides golfers and instructors with a platform to streamline communication for instruction and lesson bookings. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

2. Information Collection

Personal Information: We collect the following personal information from users: name, email address, and phone number. This information is collected during the sign-up process.

Non-Personal Information: We do not collect non-personal information such as usage data or device information.

3. Data Usage

Purpose: The personal information we collect is used to create user accounts, allowing golf instructors to identify individuals reaching out to them.

Marketing: We do not use your data for marketing purposes.

4. Data Sharing

Third Parties: We do not share your personal data with third parties.

Data Transfers: We do not transfer your data across borders.

5. Data Security

We implement access controls to protect your data and ensure its security.

6. User Rights

Access and Control: Users can access, correct, or delete their personal data by signing in and editing their information. Users can also delete their data at any time.

Opt-Out: Users can opt out of data collection or marketing communications using a toggle option available in the app.

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not collect personal data from children under 13.

8. Policy Changes

We will notify users of any changes to this privacy policy by sending a push notification through our message board and emailing all registered users.

9. Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact us at: support@tuneup.golf.
